# VoiceFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This Policy explains how Felipe Avinzano, an individual domiciled in Florida, United States ("Felipe"), handles information in felipe avinzano VoiceFlow. Contact: `legal@felipeavinzano.com`.

## 1. Local design

VoiceFlow is a desktop application with no product account or backend. Whisper transcription runs on your device. Felipe does not receive audio, transcriptions, dictionary entries, or history through the Application and cannot remotely access them.

VoiceFlow contains no telemetry, analytics, advertisements, ad pixels, sale or sharing of data, automatic crash reporting, or use of content to train models.

## 2. Information processed on your device

- **Audio:** captured in memory for transcription. The latest recording remains temporarily in memory for "Reprocess" until replaced by another recording or the Application closes. It is not intentionally written as an audio file.
- **Transcriptions and history:** stored in a local database when history is enabled.
- **Dictionary, microphone, and preferences:** stored in local files, including the accepted Terms version and timestamp.
- **Backups and temporary files:** local state may have a recovery copy and temporary files during safe writes.
- **Models and cache:** Whisper model files are downloaded and retained locally for later use without another download.
- **Clipboard and exports:** copying, pasting, or exporting places information in locations or applications you select, outside VoiceFlow's control.

These data are normally in the operating system's application-data directory for `felipe avinzano VoiceFlow`; the exact path appears in Support/Diagnostics. The model cache is in that directory under `models`.

## 3. Network connections

VoiceFlow may connect to:

- **Hugging Face:** to download Whisper models and related files. Hugging Face and its delivery networks receive ordinary connection metadata such as IP address, time, requested files, and HTTP client data. VoiceFlow does not attach audio, transcriptions, history, or dictionary entries.
- **GitHub:** to check for and, on supported releases, download updates from GitHub Releases, and to open voluntary support. GitHub receives ordinary metadata such as IP address, time, version or client, and requested resources. Update checks contain no audio, transcriptions, history, or dictionary entries.

Those providers handle metadata under their own policies. Local transcription can continue offline once a model is downloaded, although updates and new downloads will be unavailable.

## 4. Voluntary support

The support button may open GitHub Issues. Issues may be public; do not post recordings, transcriptions, personal data, or confidential information. The copyable diagnostic is designed not to include transcriptions, but review it before sharing. Information you voluntarily send through GitHub or email is used to respond and maintain product security or quality and retained as needed for those purposes or legal obligations.

## 5. Retention and deletion

Local data remain until you delete them. You may delete individual history entries or use **Support > Delete my local personal data**, which closes the database, removes history, dictionary, preferences, legal acceptance, backups, and known temporary files, and restarts the Application in a clean state. This does not delete installed binaries or the model cache; models have a separate Support control.

Uninstalling may preserve the data directory for recovery. Use the deletion action first if you want to erase local personal data, then uninstall. Felipe cannot recover deleted local data or erase it remotely.

## 6. Legal bases, scope, and rights

Where Felipe handles information voluntarily received through support, the legal basis may be responding to your request, performing the Terms, legitimate support and security interests, and legal obligations. Strictly local activity remains under your control and is not transmitted to Felipe.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, object, restrict, or port information Felipe actually holds and to complain to a regulator. Email `legal@felipeavinzano.com`. We will verify requests proportionately. We cannot provide data we never received or access data stored only on your device.

We do not sell or share personal information for behavioral advertising. VoiceFlow is not directed to anyone under 18.

## 7. Security, transfers, and changes

The local architecture reduces transfers, but no software or device is completely secure. Protect your operating-system account, disk, backups, and clipboard. Information you send to support may be processed in the United States and by the provider you select, subject to safeguards required by applicable law.

We may update this Policy as the product or law changes. Informational changes will be notified appropriately rather than presented artificially as consent. If a change legally requires a specific choice, it will be requested separately.
